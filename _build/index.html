VocPatterns

This website is a catalogue of patterns for the modelling and delivery of SKOS vocabularies, very much modelled on Leigh Dodds’ excellent Linked Data Patterns.

Aim

The aim of this catalogue is to list, and then provide answers for, a series of common questions we’ve seen in a decade of working with vocabularies.
